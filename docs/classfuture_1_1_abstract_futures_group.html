◆ status()

template<typename F >
FutureStatus future::AbstractFuturesGroup< F >::status ( ) const
inline

Compute a global status for this FuturesGroup, based on the status of each of its futures.

This will return:

  • FutureStatus::READY if ALL its futures are ready
  • FutureStatus::ERROR if ANY of its futures is in error
  • FutureStatus::INVALID if ANY of its futures is invalid
  • FutureStatus::NOT_READY if other cases, typically when no future is in error or invalid, and maybe some future are ready but not all, i.e. at least one future is not ready.
